Estimator

Location: Indiana

Job Description:

We are looking for a full-time Estimator to join our team. The ideal candidate will have a strong understanding of engineering and construction, as well as experience with takeoffs.

Responsibilities:

The primary responsibility of the Estimator will be to prepare accurate and detailed estimates for construction projects. This will involve gathering information from the project team, including architects, engineers, and contractors. The Estimator will then use this information to create a budget for the project, which will include the cost of materials, labor, and equipment. The Estimator will also be responsible for communicating with the project team and stakeholders to ensure that the estimate is accurate and meets the project's goals. The Estimator will also be responsible for tracking the progress of the project and making adjustments to the estimate as needed.

Qualifications:

  • Bachelor's degree in engineering or a related field
  • 5+ years of experience in estimating for construction projects
  • Strong understanding of engineering and construction principles
  • Excellent communication and interpersonal skills
  • Proficiency in Microsoft Office Suite
  • Experience with takeoff software
