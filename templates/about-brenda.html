Hi, I’m Brenda Patterson, often called "The Headhunter with Skin in the Game." My journey in recruiting began in 1988, but it took on a deeper purpose when life presented me with profound challenges.

In 1994, my daughter was diagnosed with Cystic Fibrosis (CF). In 2006, my son was diagnosed with Type 1 Diabetes. These moments filled me with fear, but they also ignited a relentless drive to make a difference.

The day after my daughter’s diagnosis, I found Dr. Steve Shak, the discoverer of Pulmozyme (Genentech’s breakthrough drug for CF). A week later, I connected with Frank DeFord, whose daughter had passed away from CF, and later met Boomer Esiason, whose son Gunnar has CF. Their guidance reshaped my perspective and fueled my mission: to be the very best recruiter in pharma, biotech, and medtech—because hiring the right people changes lives

Results-Driven Recruiting with Purpose

Great recruiters don’t just fill positions—they drive innovation and progress. Over the years, I’ve built a reputation for delivering exceptional talent to companies at the forefront of medical advancements

  • Proven Recruiting Expertise: Successfully placed hundreds of candidates across 32 states, ensuring top talent meets the needs of leading pharma, biotech, and medtech companies.
  • Industry Recognition: Member of the prestigious Pinnacle Society (a consortium of the top 75 recruiters in the U.S.)
  • Leadership & Training Former President of the Indiana Search and Staffing Association and mentor to numerous top recruiter
  • Speaking & Influence: Speaker at industry conferences, sharing insights on hiring strategies that create real impact

While these accolades are humbling, what truly matters is the difference I make. I’m here to connect the right talent with the right companies—so together, we can help patients live longer, healthier lives.

Recruiting is More Than a Job - It's a Mission

Every morning, I wake up driven by the same purpose that started my journey: to make a tangible impact in healthcare by finding the best talent for the most critical roles. My company is built on passion, commitment, and an unmatched ability to deliver results.

Let's connect and build something that truly matters!
