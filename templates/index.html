About JBK SEARCH

JBK SEARCH (JBK) is a premier recruitment firm recognized for its expertise, professionalism, and unwavering dedication to both client and candidate success. With a well-established reputation in the pharmaceutical, biotech and medical device industries, we have committed over two decades helping companies secure top talent and implement strategic hiring solutions.

Founded in 1999 by Brenda Patterson, an award-winning recruitment expert, JBK was built on a vision to connect top-tier talent with companies whose work transforms lives. We believe that every placement has the potential to impact patient care, and we take that responsibility seriously.

“Sometimes you develop a passion for something because of some personal experience. That has been my case. What a profound responsibility it is to do this work.” - Brenda Patterson, Founder & CEO
